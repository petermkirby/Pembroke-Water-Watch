Get in touch

Questions, tips, a brown-water incident, or a water concern you want a second opinion on — we read every message.

Other ways to reach us

Email: hello@pembrokewaterwatch.org

Response time: We're volunteer-run, so please allow a few days for a reply.

We don't maintain a physical office — this is a group of Pembroke residents doing this alongside our day jobs, not a staffed organization.

For official Water Division business (billing, service issues, reporting discolored water directly to the town), contact the Pembroke DPW Water Division at (781) 709-1425.

Pembroke Water Watch is an independent, volunteer-run community water quality initiative. We are not affiliated with the Town of Pembroke or the Pembroke Water Division. Data referenced on this site is drawn from public EPA and Massachusetts DEP records, cross-checked against multiple independent trackers where possible.
